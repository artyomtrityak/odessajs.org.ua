ABOUT ODESSAJS 2021

OdessaJS is an annual meeting of JavaScript community. This year we invite you to join our conference at True Man Hot Boat Club. Conference format - Open Air Seaside Weekend. 35+ speakers, discussions, tech corners and Q&As.
