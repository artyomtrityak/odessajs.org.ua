Three-day conference on in-depth JavaScript Technologies

Welcome to OdessaJS conference - one of the biggest JavaScript conference
next to the Black Sea in Ukraine.

This year it will be already the 6th conference.

Conference is full of strong technical talks and workshops, carefully selected by our Program Committee. To connect attendees together and drive more interesting meetings, every year we prepare big afterparty, a lot of fun and tech activities during the conference.

The sunny Odessa, warm sea, the summerly and friendly atmosphere at OdessaJS are memories and knowledge to remember all following year after the conference.

July 6th

  • Full day of extensively trainings
  • Ice breaker pre-party with lightning talks

July 7th

  • Morning yoga on the beach
  • Full-day of 2 tracks technical speeches and tech-corners for open discussions on trended topics
  • Big afterparty with music, food and easy networking

Location
