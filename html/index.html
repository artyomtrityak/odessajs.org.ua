Diversity scholarships

We believe in equality and equal possibilities for anyone and everyone. That’s why we seek to gather together at the Conference the most diverse group of people possible. This could become achievable thanks to the companies and individuals who donate to our Scholarship Program.

If you’d like to get involved as an individual, please choose any of the Diversity Support Tickets or contact us at any time at odessajsconf@gmail.com. If you’re a company representative please contact us and we provide you with different options.

Anyone from an underrepresented group in tech is invited to apply for a scholarship. This includes, but is not limited to: women, people of color, LGBTQIA+ people, disabled people, and people facing economic or social hardships.

Applications for our scholarship program are open till June 15, 2018.
